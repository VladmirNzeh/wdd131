Impact on Society

Corruption in the justice system has far-reaching effects on society. It erodes public trust, leads to economic losses, and fosters social instability.

Economic Impact

Studies show significant financial losses due to corruption, affecting both local and national economies.

Social Impact

The breakdown of trust in legal institutions leads to public disillusionment and hinders social progress.
