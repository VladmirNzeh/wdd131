Forms of Corruption

Corruption in Nigeria manifests in various forms, undermining the integrity of the justice system. Below is a breakdown of key types of corruption:

Judicial Bribery

Bribery within the judiciary undermines fair trials and erodes public trust.

Police Brutality

Excessive use of force and bribery within law enforcement create significant barriers to justice.

Electoral Fraud

Manipulation during elections contributes to an overall climate of distrust in governance.
