Solutions & Call to Action

It is time for collective action. The following solutions are proposed to help restore integrity and accountability:

  • Report Corruption: Use secure channels to report incidents.
  • Advocate for Reform: Join local and national advocacy groups.
  • Engage with Policy Makers: Voice your concerns and support for transparency.
  • Educate the Public: Share verified information and support community outreach.
