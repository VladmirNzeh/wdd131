About the Justice System

Nigeria's justice system was designed to ensure fairness and transparency. However, over the years, systemic corruption and political interference have compromised its effectiveness.

Historical Context

Once a modern framework inspired by colonial-era structures, the system has been undermined by various challenges. The intended promise of justice has, in many cases, been replaced by inefficiency and corruption.

Current Challenges

  • Political interference and bribery
  • Lack of transparency in legal processes
  • Inadequate funding and resources
  • Corruption within law enforcement agencies
